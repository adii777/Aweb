Eat Healthy,Live Longer.

Weight Loss

The most essential change to make in your diet for quick weight loss is to cut down on refined carbs. Cut back on sugars, starches and carbohydrates.

Your each meal should include a protien source, vegetables, fat and small portion of complex carbohydrates such as whole grains.

Protien

Protien reduces craving for food to a good extent.

Vital protien sources include:

  • Meat:beef, chicken, pork, lamb
  • Seafood: salmon, trout,shrimp
  • Eggs
  • Plant-based protien: beans,legumes,quinoa,tempeh and tofu.

Vegetables

Green leafy vegetables have low carbs. These include:

  • Spinach
  • Cauliflower
  • Broccoli
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Swiss Chards

Healthy Fats

While on a diet, your body still needs healthy fats. Olive oil and Avocado Oil should be included in the meals.

Immunity Boost

Prevention is better than cure & having a strong immune system is the best form of prevention. These foods can boost your immunity exorbitantly.

Almonds

Almonds contain Vitamin-E and a good amount healthy fats. Vitamin-E is the best source for the prevention and cure of cold. Half a cup serving of almonds daily can fulfill our daily needs.

Red Bell Pepper

Bell Peppers are 3 times richer in Vitamin-C. These may help you keep a healthy skin & healthy eyes.

Citrus Fruits

Rich in Vitamin-C. Increases the white blood cell count and helps in fighting infections

Spinach

Packed with antioxidants and beta carotene

Garlic

It slows down the hardening of arteries and also helps in lowering the blood pressure. It's immune boosting properties comes from a heavy concentration of sulfur containing compound, allicin.

Papaya

It's loaded with vitamin C and also contains decent amount of pottasium, magnesium and folate which are valuable for the overall health.

Yogurt

Yogurt is a good source of Vitamin-D. Try to take plain yogurt rather than flavoured or sugared.
