Eat Healthy,Live Longer.

Weight Loss

The most essential change to make in your diet for quick weight loss is to cut down on refined carbs. Cut back on sugars, starches and carbohydrates.

Your each meal should include a protien source, vegetables, fat and small portion of complex carbohydrates such as whole grains.

Protien

Protien reduces craving for food to a good extent.

Vital protien sources include:

  • Meat:beef, chicken, pork, lamb
  • Seafood: salmon, trout,shrimp
  • Eggs
  • Plant-based protien: beans,legumes,quinoa,tempeh and tofu.

Vegetables

Green leafy vegetables have low carbs. These include:

  • Spinach
  • Cauliflower
  • Broccoli
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Swiss Chards

Healthy Fats

While on a diet, your body still needs healthy fats. Olive oil and Avocado Oil should be included in the meals.
